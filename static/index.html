About - The JetLagJelly Team

What is JetLagJelly?

JetLagJelly is a semester-long project made by a team of Computer Science students at SUNY Oswego. For professionals around the world who struggle to find common time slots for scheduling team meetings, JetlagJelly is a Google plug-in that helps users avoid the headache that goes into scheduling international meetings. Unlike alternatives, such as Doodle and standard Google Calendar, our product finds an optimal time that is convenient for all meeting participants.

Additional Credit:

Our back-end was built using Java. Our front-end was written primarily in Google Apps Script for JetLagJelly to work as a Google Calendar plugin. JavaScript, HTML, and CSS were also used in the creation of this page and a prototype frontend(which was later scrapped). We used MongoDB for our database, and Google API Services allowed us to make our program a Google Calendar plug-in. The credits also go to SpringBoot and Docker, which were utilized for the creation and deployment of our product. Thanks to Digital Ocean for hosting our server!
